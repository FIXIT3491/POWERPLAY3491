---------
GamePad 1
---------
Left Bumper/Right Bumper = Grabber

Left Joystick X = Strafing

Left Joystick Y Forwards/Backwards

Right Joystick X = Turning

---------
GamePad 2
---------
Left Bumper/Right Bumper = Grabber

X = Slide Floor

Y = Slide Low Pole

B = Slide Mid Pole

A = Slide Hi Pole

Left Joystick Y = Slide Up/Down
